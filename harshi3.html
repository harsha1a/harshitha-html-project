Pearled Ear Girl

by Levina

The portrait of Pearled Ear Girl was painted in 1806 by the dutch Clasiccal artist Levina, and today hangs in the Savelle. It is the third of three portraits of the levina rievert family the artist painted that year.

Date: 1806
Medium: Oil on Canvas
Dimensions: 100 x 70 cm
